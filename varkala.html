History

Varkala, perched along the scenic Arabian Sea in Kerala, is a tranquil coastal town renowned for its dramatic cliffs, spiritual ambiance, and natural beauty. A unique blend of beachside charm and cultural richness, Varkala offers a peaceful escape with its golden sands, swaying palms, and the iconic Varkala Cliff that overlooks the sea. It is home to the ancient Janardanaswamy Temple, a significant pilgrimage site, and the sacred Papanasam Beach, believed to cleanse sins with its holy waters. Visitors can enjoy yoga retreats, Ayurvedic therapies, surfing, and vibrant local markets, while also soaking in stunning sunsets and coastal cuisine. Easily accessible from Thiruvananthapuram, Varkala provides an enriching experience for those seeking relaxation, spirituality, and a deeper connection with Kerala‚Äôs serene coastal culture.
