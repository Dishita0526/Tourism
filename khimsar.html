History

KHIMSAR, A HIDDEN GEM IN THE HEART OF RAJASTHAN, INDIA, IS STEEPED IN THE LEGACY OF THE RAJPUTANA WARRIORS AND DESERT KINGDOMS. FOUNDED IN THE 16TH CENTURY BY RAO KARAMSJI, THE EIGHTH SON OF RAO JODHA‚ÄîTHE FOUNDER OF JODHPUR‚ÄîKHIMSAR FLOURISHED AS A STRATEGIC MILITARY OUTPOST ON THE ANCIENT TRADE ROUTES BETWEEN MARWAR AND THE THAR DESERT. DOMINATED BY THE MAJESTIC KHIMSAR FORT, WHICH SEAMLESSLY BLENDS MILITARY ARCHITECTURE WITH ROYAL SPLENDOR, THE TOWN REFLECTS A HERITAGE OF VALOR, LUXURY, AND ENDURANCE. SURROUNDED BY SAND DUNES AND REMOTE VILLAGES, KHIMSAR OFFERS A TRANQUIL YET VIVID GLIMPSE INTO RAJASTHAN'S DESERT CULTURE, WHERE HISTORY WHISPERS THROUGH WIND-CARVED WALLS AND TIME STANDS STILL UNDER STARRY SKIES.
